About me

Hi! My name is Dorien and I am originally from Belgium. After graduating as a Bio-Engineer, I moved to Washington DC to start a PhD in Neuroscience. For the past 8 years, I have been involved with quantitative, data-driven research.
I recently graduated from Le Wagon's 10 week intensive Data Science Bootcamp in Berlin, where I deep-dived into state-of-the-art data science concepts, including data sourcing and processing, building machine and deep learning models, developing prediction pipelines pipelines and the producing data applications.

Projects

Lung Pollution

AI/ML project to analyse the impact of air pollution on CoViD-19, including a real-time predictor. This project was developed with a team of 3 colleages during the two final weeks of our data science bootcamp.

Microplastic Detection
