About me

Hi! My name is Dorien and I am originally from Belgium. I hold an MSc degree in Bio-Engineering (KU Leuven, Belgium) and a PhD degree in Neuroscience (National Institutes of Health, USA).

With 8 years of experience in quantitative, data-driven research, I enjoy turning complex data into meaningful insights.

I recently decided to upgrade my coding skills by completing Le Wagon's 10 week intensive Data Science Bootcamp in Berlin, where I deep-dived into state-of-the-art data science concepts, including data sourcing and processing, building machine and deep learning models, developing prediction pipelines and data engineering.
