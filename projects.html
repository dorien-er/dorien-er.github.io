My data science projects

Air pollution can have serious impacts on our respiratory health. Here, we analyze the impact of air pollution on CoViD-19 alongside other disease modulators such as vaccination rate and population density. To this end, we developed interactive data visualisations, built machine learning pipelines and deployed prediction models as a web application. This project was developed from idea to web app together with 3 colleages as a graduation project during the last 2 weeks of Le Wagon's Data Science Bootcamp.
